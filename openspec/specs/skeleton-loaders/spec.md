# Capability: Skeleton Loaders

## Purpose
Improves perceived performance and UX by providing visual placeholders while content is being fetched.

## Requirements

### Requirement: Theme-Aware Skeletons
The system SHALL provide skeleton placeholders that adapt their appearance (shimmer color/gradient) to the current active theme (Finished/Blueprint).

### Requirement: Multiple Shapes
The skeleton system SHALL support different shapes (text, card, circle) to match the expected content structure.
